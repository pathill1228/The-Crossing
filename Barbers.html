Benji

My name is Benjamin Gonzalez, and I’ve been cutting hair since I was 15 years old. What started as something I did for friends quickly turned into a craft I fell in love with. Over the years, I’ve dedicated myself to mastering clean fades, beard work, and modern men’s cuts that bring out each client’s best look.

I take pride in precision and consistency. Every cut I do is intentional — it’s not just about the haircut, it’s about giving someone the confidence to walk out feeling sharp, refreshed, and put together.
